About Me

For me, the pursuit (…of knowledge, … of expertise, …of skills, …of why, …of the solution to the puzzle) is more than a means to an end. The personal growth, knowledge, new skills, and perspective gained from the process, project, or work are a part of the result. Because of this awareness, I’m not satisfied with pursuing only the things I know and do well. I’m far more satisfied doing work that requires me to apply my experiences to unfamiliar areas.
